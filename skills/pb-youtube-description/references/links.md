## HiRose tracking slug conventions

Format: `<masterclass-url>?el=<video-slug>`

Slug rules:
- lowercase
- kebab-case
- derived from filename or topic, whichever is more readable
- examples:
  - `mouthpiece-test-final` (mouthpiece comparison video)
  - `stop-buying-mouthpieces` (older mouthpieces video)
  - `embouchure-converter` (the $36K converter)

When unsure, ask Timo. Don't invent a slug that diverges from his naming convention.
