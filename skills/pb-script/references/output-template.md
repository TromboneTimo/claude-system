## Rules for the template

- **Always show the date.** Helps when comparing menus across weeks.
- **Always cite sources by file path.** Timo can verify any quote in seconds.
- **Always label the anchor** (YT-WINNER-PATTERN vs LENS-NAME). The 1-2 / 3-4 split is the whole point of this skill, the labels make it visible.
- **Verbatim quotes only.** If you can't find a real quote in the corpus, weaken the idea or swap it. Never invent a quote.
- **Title variations are required.** Three is the minimum. They give Timo something to A/B against the working title.
- **End with the "do not film yet" reminder.** Project rule.
