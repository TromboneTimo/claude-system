Do not use em dashes (&mdash; or —) in any copy, marketing content, or generated text. The user considers them a strong AI writing signal.

**Why:** Em dashes are overused by AI-generated text and immediately flag content as machine-written.

**How to apply:** Use hyphens, periods, commas, or rewrite sentences to avoid the need for em dashes entirely. Applies to all output: landing pages, social posts, blog content, emails, everything.
