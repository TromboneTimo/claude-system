# ICP Segmentation Is the Default Axis, Not Angle Variety

**Rule:** When Timo asks for multiple Power Content ideas, the DEFAULT output is ONE IDEA PER ICP SEGMENT. Not multiple angle variations on one topic.

**Why:** On 2026-04-12, Timo asked for "5 different angles based on my ICP" which are: music coaches, business owners, AI lovers, view-rich musicians not monetizing. I inverted this and generated 15 angle variations on ONE topic (the designer/marketer angle) because that was fresh in the conversation. He was rightly pissed. His audience is NOT monolithic. Each ICP has different psychology, different proof, different hooks.

**How to apply:**
- Default `/power-content` invocation = spawn ONE persona per ICP segment, not 5 angle personas on one topic.
- Only do angle deep-dive (Grievance/Contrarian/Proof/Pattern/Psychological) when Timo EXPLICITLY names a topic to dive into.
- If ambiguous, ASK: "Per-ICP (default) or topic deep-dive?" Don't assume.
- When generating any content for CC, audit whether the output serves all his ICPs or just one. The $47/mo Library targets different ICPs than the $500/mo coaching.
- Read audience.md ICP Tiers table BEFORE generating anything. Tiers: Ceiling (music coaches running ads), Sweet Spot (coaches with offers), Growth (musicians with small followings), Expanded (business owners/content creators), Floor (anyone building systems).

**ICPs to segment by (as of 2026-04-12):**
1. Music Coaches (Harrison-tier)
2. Business Owners (non-music)
3. AI Lovers (tech-curious, prompt-hackers)
4. View-Rich Cash-Poor Musicians
5. Escaping the Orchestra Artists (primary CC ICP)

**Related lesson:** Depth in the wrong dimension is still failure. 15 variations on one topic looked thorough but was wrong axis. Thoroughness must match intent.
