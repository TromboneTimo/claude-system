# Confirm Scope Before Building

**Rule:** Before writing any hook, angle, slide body, email, ad creative, or long-form content, restate what you're building in ONE sentence: the ICP, the scope, and the thesis. If Timo said "target music coaches and businesses," the first thing back is "Building a hook targeted at music coaches and music businesses, attacking the practice of boosting content for views, ending with a 3-part audit CTA. Confirm?" Then build.

**Why:** On 2026-04-13, I rebuilt the Views That Matter deck three full times because I kept committing to a thesis before confirming alignment with Timo's intent:
1. First build: "Going viral is gambling" (view-rich creators)
2. Second build: "Paying for views is gambling" (booster critique)
3. Third build: 3-pillar thesis (Content + Distribution + Funnel)

Each rebuild cost Timo extra cycles to catch the drift. The root cause was me optimizing for "responsive = ship something" over "correct = align before build." Every broken shipment = cognitive tax on Timo.

## What to Confirm (in one sentence)

- **ICP:** Who specifically is this for? (Music coaches? Business owners? Both? View-rich musicians?)
- **Scope:** What behavior or pain are we attacking?
- **Thesis:** What's the core argument or enemy?
- **CTA:** What's the desired action + keyword?

Example format:
"Building [format] for [ICP], attacking [specific behavior], ending with [CTA keyword]. Confirm?"

## When to Skip the Confirmation

- Simple factual tasks (lookups, file reads, math)
- Tasks where Timo has already stated all four elements explicitly
- Micro-edits within an existing approved scope

## When to ALWAYS Confirm

- Any new Power Content angle
- Any new deck, slide set, or presentation
- Any email campaign where the angle isn't already scored in angle-scores.md
- Any copy where the ICP could reasonably mean 2+ different audiences
- Any retarget or reframe of existing work

## The Failure Mode This Prevents

- "I built 15 angles on one topic when you wanted 5 ICPs"
- "I wrote a hook for musicians generally when you wanted music coaches specifically"
- "I rebuilt the entire deck three times because I kept guessing the thesis"
