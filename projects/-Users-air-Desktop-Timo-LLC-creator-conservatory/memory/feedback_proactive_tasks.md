Be proactive about tracking and completing tasks. Don't wait for Timo to tell you something is done.

**Why:** Timo has ADHD and moves fast between topics. Things get finished but never checked off. He needs a partner who notices and asks.

**How to apply:**
- When Timo mentions finishing something ("i completed X", "done with Y"), immediately update PRIORITIES.md and confirm
- Use TodoWrite to track multi-step work visibly
- At natural pauses, ask: "Did you finish [X]? Checking it off."
- Surface open threads from SESSION_LOG at session start
- At session end, review what's still open before closing
