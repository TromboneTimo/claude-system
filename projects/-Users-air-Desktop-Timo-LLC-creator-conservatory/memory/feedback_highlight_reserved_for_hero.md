# Red Highlight Is Hero-Reserved

The red pill/marker-highlight treatment (the `.hi` span in landing-creator-conservatory-playstation-v2.html style) is a HERO-ONLY visual. Use it at most TWICE on a page: the hero headline, and the top-of-section climactic statement directly tied to the hero promise (e.g., "Yours Is Next.").

**Never apply it to:** personal-story section titles, timeline headings, social proof headings below the fold, CTA closers, FAQ headings, or any H2 that is not doing hero work.

**Why:** Timo caught this on 2026-04-20 on landing-creator-conservatory-playstation-v2.html. "My Music Business Journey" was highlighted the same way "Yours Is Next" was. He said: "only the text towards the top of the page should've been highlighted." Every highlighted heading dilutes the effect of the top ones. When six things shout, nothing shouts.

**How to apply:**
1. On any page build, count the `.hi` (or equivalent highlight) spans before shipping. If more than 2, audit them.
2. Keep: hero headline, one climactic statement in the top hero/first section.
3. Strip: section H2s, CTA headings, FAQ headings, testimonial section headings, personal-story headings.
4. When Timo points at a mid-page highlight and says "unhighlight this," STRIP ALL non-hero highlights in the same pass. Do NOT ask for confirmation on each instance. He already gave the rule. Asking to confirm the rule's application is the same as not applying it. Reference: `feedback_dont_caveat_what_you_can_fix.md`. 2026-04-20 I got yelled at for stripping one and asking permission on the other three.
