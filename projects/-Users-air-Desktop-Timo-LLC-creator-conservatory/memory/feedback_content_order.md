When updating pages or SOPs from a video transcript, follow the video's actual order. If the video starts with "install these tools," the page should start with downloads/install, not bury it at Step 12.

**Why:** Timo called this out directly. The video literally opens with "install Claude Code and VS Code" but I put downloads last. That's backwards and makes the guide not match what viewers just watched.

**How to apply:** Before restructuring any content from a transcript, map the transcript's sequence first. The page flow should mirror the video flow so readers who just watched the video see the same order.
