Never write "free strategy call", "free coaching call", "free consultation", or similar. Timo calls these phrases "stupid" and marketing-guru-coded.

Default CTA copy:
- Button: "Book a chat with Tim" or "Chat with Tim" (compact)
- Form title: "Book a chat with Tim"
- Section heading: "Book a chat with Tim"
- Never: "strategy call", "coaching call", "discovery call", "consultation", "book your free call"

**Why:** Timo is anti-guru. "Strategy call" signals bro-marketer energy and he actively opposes that positioning. Plain human language ("a chat") feels honest and lowers the perceived-pressure barrier. This aligns with the broader brand voice rule in CLAUDE.md: never sound like a marketing guru.

**How to apply:** On any landing page, button, nav CTA, email footer, form title, or body copy, use "chat with Tim" variants. If a synonym is needed, prefer neutral words like "a call," "a quick chat," or "a 15-min conversation." Never reinsert "strategy" or "coaching" as a modifier on the call itself.
