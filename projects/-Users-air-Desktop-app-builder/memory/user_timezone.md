User is located in Japan — timezone is JST (UTC+9).
When scheduling anything (YouTube publish times, calendar events, etc.), always convert from JST to UTC.
Example: 11:30 PM JST = 14:30 UTC same day.

**Why:** Got burned scheduling a YouTube video with wrong UTC offset — video went public at 3:30 PM next day instead of 11:30 PM that night.
