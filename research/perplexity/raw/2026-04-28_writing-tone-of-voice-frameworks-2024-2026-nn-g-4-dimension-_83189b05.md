### Nielsen Norman Group Four Dimensions of Tone of Voice
**Structure**: Bipolar scales across four dimensions—**Funny vs. Serious**, **Formal vs. Casual**, **Respectful vs. Irreverent**, **Enthusiastic vs. Matter-of-fact**—analyzed qualitatively to profile content tone.[1][5][8]  
**Inputs required**: Existing brand content samples for evaluation along each dimension; no formal workshop specified.[1][3]  
**Output document looks like**: A simple profile (e.g., "Casual-Serious-Respectful-Enthusiastic") or visual scale placement, often expanded into 16 profiles like "Earnest Ally" (CSRE).[1]  
**Iteration process**: Not detailed in core framework; extensions suggest re-evaluating content against profiles post-testing to refine (e.g., Oli West's 2023 adaptation).[1]
