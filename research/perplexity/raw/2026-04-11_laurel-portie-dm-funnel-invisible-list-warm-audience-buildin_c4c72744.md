# Laurel Portie's Audience-Building Strategies

## The Invisible List

Laurel Portie's **"invisible list"** refers to a retargeting audience built from people who engage with your content on social platforms without explicitly opting into an email list[4][5]. These are viewers who watch 25% or more of your video content on Facebook or similar platforms—they're tracked by the platform's pixel but remain "invisible" in traditional email metrics[5]. This segment consists of "a highly qualified group that has been gradually accustomed and primed through regular exposure to your expertise," making them highly receptive to future promotions without requiring them to have taken an explicit action like signing up[4].

## Building Warm Audiences Without Email

Portie's approach creates two simultaneous audiences: a **visible list (email)** and an **invisible list (retargeting)**[7]. The invisible list strategy specifically allows her to nurture warm audiences without relying solely on email by continuously retargeting people based on their video consumption behavior on the platform itself[5]. This means she builds a warm audience through repeated content exposure on Facebook/social media, where the platform tracks viewers and allows her to show them follow-up ads based on their engagement history.
