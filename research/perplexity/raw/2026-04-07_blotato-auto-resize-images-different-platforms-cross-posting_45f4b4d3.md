# Blotato auto resize images different platforms cross-posting. Does Blotato automatically resize or adapt images for different social media platforms? For example if I upload a 1080

## Key findings

**No, Blotato does not automatically resize or adapt a single uploaded image (like a 1080x1350 Instagram carousel image) for optimal aspect ratios across platforms such as LinkedIn, X/Twitter, or Facebook during cross-posting.**[1][3][4]

Blotato supports cross-platform publishing, including to Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Pinterest, and TikTok, but requires manual or template-based handling for platform-specific image formats.[1][3][4] Users can upload custom images (max 20MB per image) directly into video or carousel templates via advanced options, such as "Upload Image" for each slide, but Blotato does not auto-resize or reformat them to match platform optima (e.g., Instagram's 1080x1350 to Twitter's 16:9).[3][6] For multi-format needs, integrations like n8n workflows with Abyssale generate separate versions for each platform (e.g., Instagram post/story, LinkedIn feed, Twitter/X post) before publishing via Blotato nodes.[1][5]
