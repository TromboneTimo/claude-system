## Key findings

**For clients who resist structured processes like templates, coaches and consultants deliver SOPs most effectively through recorded videos (e.g., Loom walkthroughs), live recordings of their actual tasks, and light, skimmable checklists built around their real workflows rather than static documents.**[1][2]
