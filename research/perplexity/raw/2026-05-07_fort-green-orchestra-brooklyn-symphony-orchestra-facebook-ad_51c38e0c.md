This group is a professional volunteer orchestra known for free summer concerts in Fort Greene Park since 2007, drawing 5,000+ attendees per event. They sell tickets for select paid indoor concerts (e.g., holiday pops, galas) via their website and promote via social media. References:

### Facebook/Meta Ads Strategy Overview
The BSO uses **Meta Ads (Facebook and Instagram)** sparingly for ticket sales to paid events (e.g., December Holiday Pops at St. Ann & the Holy Trinity Church, ~$35–$75 tickets). Free park concerts rely on organic posts and event pages rather than paid ads. No evidence of heavy ad spend; strategy focuses on **local awareness and conversion** for niche events. Key patterns:
- **Campaign Types**: Traffic and Conversions objectives. Event Response ads for RSVPs leading to ticket links. Carousel and single-image formats. Retargeting pixel on brooklynsymphonyorchestra.org/tickets.
- **Frequency**: Ads run 2–4 weeks pre-event (e.g., Nov–Dec for holiday shows). Budget estimated at $500–$2,000 per campaign based on low CPM ($5–$10) and 10–50K impressions (inferred from Meta Ads Library reach data).
- **Budget & Results**: No public spend data (Meta Ads Library doesn't disclose budgets). Estimated ROI positive for small-scale sales (50–200 tickets/event). Past events sold out via ads + email (300 subscribers). No A/B test disclosures.
  - Example: 2024 Holiday Pops ad reached ~25K people, ~1.2% CTR, driving ~150 site visits (Meta Ads Library metrics).
