## Top Tools for Authentic Carousel Creation

**Canva AI**
Canva combines generative AI with extensive editing capabilities to create custom-looking carousels.[1] The platform includes thousands of Instagram-specific carousel templates, but its strength lies in customization through the **Brand Kit feature**, which automatically applies your colors, fonts, and logos across all designs to maintain visual consistency.[1] The **Magic Resize function** instantly adjusts visuals to fit every Instagram format, while AI editing tools like Magic Eraser, Background Remover, and Magic Edit allow you to customize designs beyond template defaults.[1] Canva AI also lets you upload your own images alongside text prompts for more personalized outputs.[1]

**Pricing:** Free tier available; Canva Pro at $120/year; Canva Team at $100/person/year[1]
