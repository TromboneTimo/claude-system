# What integrations does Skool.com support? Does it work with Zapier, Make.com, or any automation tools? What data can you access programmatically from Skool communities - member lis

## Key findings

**Skool.com supports native integrations with Zapier (Pro plan only), Make.com (via private app), Pabbly Connect, and tracking tools like Hyros; it also works indirectly with platforms like Kajabi, GoHighLevel (via CRM Connector), and thousands of apps through these automation tools.** Popular Zapier connections include Stripe, Google Sheets, ClickFunnels, Airtable, Slack, Shopify, and Webhooks by Zapier[2][3]. Make.com offers verified triggers/actions like "Invite a New Member" and custom workflows[1][4]. Pabbly enables connections to over 1000 apps for CRM, sales, and marketing[9].

**Programmatically accessible data via these tools includes member details (name, email), membership questions/answers, paid member info, and actions like inviting members or unlocking courses.** Specific triggers/actions:
- Send paid members' name/email to CRM[3][5][6].
- Forward membership questions/answers to CRM/Google Sheets/Airtable[2][3][6].
- Membership requests (as "Lead" events in Hyros, requiring email via questions)[7].
No public API exists, so access relies on these plugins; posts and full analytics are not explicitly mentioned as accessible[4].
