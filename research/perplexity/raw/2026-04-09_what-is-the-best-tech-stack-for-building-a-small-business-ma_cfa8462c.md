# What is the best tech stack for building a small business marketing dashboard in 2025-2026 that pulls data from Shopify API, Amazon Seller Central API, Google Analytics, Instagram

## Key findings

**For a solo developer building a small business marketing dashboard in 2025-2026 that integrates Shopify API, Amazon Seller Central API, Google Analytics, Instagram API, and email platforms (e.g., Klaviyo or Mailchimp), Next.js + Supabase offers the best balance of customization, cost, and scalability for a 3-4 person team.** This stack enables full control over unique API pulls and real-time features without vendor lock-in, outperforming low-code alternatives for complex, ongoing needs. Retool suits rapid prototyping but limits deep customization, while Grafana excels in visualization yet requires a separate backend. Custom-built (purely from scratch without frameworks) is overly time-intensive for a solo dev.
