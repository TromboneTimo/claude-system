# Laurel Portie diffusion of innovations marketing Rogers adoption curve ads strategy innovators early adopters. How does she apply diffusion of innovations theory to her ad strategy

## Key findings

Laurel Portié applies **diffusion of innovations theory** (popularized by Everett Rogers) to her ad strategy by tailoring messaging and content delivery to different audience segments based on their adoption stage, recognizing that people at varying points in the innovation adoption curve respond differently to information and require distinct approaches to move them from cold leads to clients.[3]
