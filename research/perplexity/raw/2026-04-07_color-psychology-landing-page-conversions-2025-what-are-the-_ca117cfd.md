# Color psychology landing page conversions 2025. What are the highest converting CTA button colors with data? What hex codes for dark professional backgrounds with light text? Best

## Key findings

**No single CTA button color universally converts the best; success depends on contrast with the surrounding background, audience, and brand, with orange, blue, red, and green appearing most frequently in high-converting examples.**[1][2]

Studies analyzing 90 high-converting CTAs found orange (for urgency and excitement), blue, red, and green as the top background colors, as they stand out on light or dark pages, often paired with white text for readability (78/90 buttons used white).[1][2] Red outperformed green by 34% (Dmix, 600 subjects), 21% (HubSpot), and 5% (VWO), though blue beat orange by 9% in one test; another chart showed blue and green leading overall.[1][3] Neil Patel's data confirms **blue and green as top performers**, with white and yellow lagging.[3] For 2025 landing pages, prioritize high contrast over specific hues—e.g., colored buttons on white/grey backgrounds—and A/B test.[1][2][4][5]
