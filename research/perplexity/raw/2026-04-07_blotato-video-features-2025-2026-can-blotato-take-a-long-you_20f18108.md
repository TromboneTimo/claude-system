# Blotato video features 2025 2026: Can Blotato take a long YouTube video and automatically clip it into short vertical videos for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts? Does B

## Key findings

**No, Blotato cannot automatically clip long YouTube videos into short vertical videos for TikTok, Instagram Reels, or YouTube Shorts.** It explicitly lacks this capability and is not designed for clipping longform content into shorts; alternatives like Opus Clip or Klap are recommended instead.[2][8]

Blotato offers limited **video editing features**, such as combining pre-existing clips, aligning images/video tracks to captions, adding text overlays, stretching elements across timelines, reordering/deleting scenes, and basic enhancements like music or voiceover integration via templates (e.g., "Combine Clips and Apply Basic Edits" or "Combine Existing Clips").[1][2][3][5] However, it does not support **cutting (trimming specific segments) or cropping (resizing/reframing) actual video files** from long videos—users must provide short clips or let AI generate new content.[2][5]

The **Blotato faceless video generator creates new videos from scratch using AI**, not by clipping existing ones. It generates videos from text inputs like scripts, articles, prompts, or podcasts by producing AI-selected/animated images, voiceovers, captions, and stock visuals, which users then edit in a timeline-based editor before exporting as MP4.[1][3][4][7] For example, you paste a script, generate a draft with images, swap them, align to voiceover captions, and export—without processing uploaded long videos.[3]
