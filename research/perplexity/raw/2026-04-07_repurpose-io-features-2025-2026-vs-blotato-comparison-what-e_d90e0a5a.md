## What Repurpose.io Does for Video Repurposing

**Core Functionality:**
Repurpose.io operates on a **Source → Action → Destination** workflow model[2]. You upload content from a source platform (like YouTube, Twitch, or a live stream), and the platform automatically repurposes and redistributes it to multiple destinations[3].

**Specific Video Repurposing Features:**

- **Automatic Resizing and Cropping:** The platform automatically resizes and optimizes videos to fit each platform perfectly[4]. For vertical content, it includes **automatic cropping to vertical format**[7].

- **Watermark Removal:** Repurpose.io automatically removes platform-specific watermarks so content appears native on each platform[2][4].

- **Direct Publishing:** Rather than just downloading files, Repurpose.io publishes directly to social platforms[7].

- **Automation Options:** You can set workflows to "Manual" (approve each post) or "Auto" (publish immediately upon detecting new content)[2].
