# Is Gemini image generation free? What are the free tier limits for gemini-2.0-flash-image and gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview models? How many free image generations per day? What i

## Key findings

**Gemini image generation is free in the free tier with daily limits varying by model, interface (e.g., app, AI Studio, API), and subscription status.** For **gemini-2.0-flash-image** (also referenced as Gemini 2.5 Flash Image or similar), the free tier allows up to **500 images per day via API** (subject to rate limits like ~2 images/minute and 1,500 total daily requests), while the Gemini app limits free users to ~100 images/day (recently reduced to ~50 in some reports).[3][1][3] For **gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview** (also called Nano Banana 2 or Gemini 3.1 Flash Image), free users are capped at **20 images per 24 hours** in the Gemini app or AI Studio web interface, with paid subscribers (AI Pro/Ultra) accessing 50–1,000 images/day.[4][5]

Free daily image generations thus range from **20–500 per model and platform**, with API offering higher quotas for compatible models like 2.0/2.5 Flash (500/day) but stricter rate pacing; app/web limits are lower (20–100/day) and can vary by demand or recent adjustments as of early 2026.[1][3][4][5][8] Preview models like gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview face tighter restrictions and may require billing for full API access.[2][7]
