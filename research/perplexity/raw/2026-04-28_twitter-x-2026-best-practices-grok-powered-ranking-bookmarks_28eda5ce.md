# Twitter X 2026 best practices - Grok-powered ranking, bookmarks as top signal, threads 8-12 tweets, X Premium 2-4x multiplier, link suppression, SimClusters topic clustering

## Key findings

### X Algorithm Signals 2025-2026
The 2026 X algorithm, powered by a Grok transformer model from xAI, prioritizes **dwell time** (time users spend on posts), **bookmarks** as a top signal (indicating high-value content users save), **replies** over retweets or likes (for sparking conversations and session depth), and **early engagement** in the first 30-60 minutes (determining 50%+ of visibility via aggressive time decay).[1][2][4][5] **Videos** receive higher weighting than text due to richer media favoritism, boosting engagement scores, while **links** face indirect suppression through delayed redirects, in-app friction, and demotion (despite official 2025 claims of removal).[1][4][5] **X Premium** provides a 2x-4x reach multiplier and credibility boost, essential for creators.[4][5] The **For You** timeline uses SimClusters (145k topic groups) for semantic relevancy, favoring in-network consistency over random posts, while **Following** prioritizes recency and follower interactions; off-topic content confuses clustering and tanks reach.[1][3][4][5] Longer **threads** outperform single posts by sustaining dwell time and replies across multiple tweets.[1][4]

### Thread vs Single-Post Strategy 2026
**Threads** dominate in 2026 for personal-brand creators, as the algorithm rewards sustained engagement over one-off posts; aim for **8-12 tweets** per thread based on creator data for optimal dwell time without fatigue.[1] Start with a **hook tweet** using **curiosity gap** (tease a secret), **contrarian** takes (challenge norms), or **list-promise** (e.g., "7 ways X changed my reach").[3] Single posts suit quick hits but lose to threads in For You distribution; space threads 1-2 days apart to avoid feed flooding.[1][3][4]

No 2026-specific threads from r/Twitter or r/SocialMediaMarketing in results, but creators report 2-4x reach drops for link-heavy posts and 3x gains from video threads with first-hour replies; Premium users note consistent For You boosts despite similar follower counts.[1][2][5] (Results lack direct Reddit links; audit subreddits for real-time 2026 posts.)

### 2026 vs 2024/2025 Deltas
- **Longer-form push**: Threads now weighted higher than single "big tweets" for session depth (vs 2024 like-favoring).[1][4]
- **Video boost**: Videos outperform text 2-3x via Grok's watch analysis (escalated from 2025).[1][5]
- **X Premium multiplier**: Now 2-4x reach (up from verification perks).[4][5]
- **New ranking model**: Grok transformer + SimClusters predict per-user engagement (vs legacy recency/likes), rewarding niche experts, bookmarks/replies, early momentum over broad appeal.[1][2][3][5]

### What NOT to Do
- **Engagement bait**: Rage bait, muted keywords, or fake prompts trigger filters and "not interested" predictions.[3]
- **Link suppression**: External links cut reach 50-90% via friction/demotion; use in-app or text-only.[1][4][5]
- **Bad ratios**: High follower-to-following imbalance, dormant first 30 mins, or off-topic posts suppress via clustering/velocity loss; avoid posting multiples rapidly.[1][2][3][4]

**Action steps**: Audit last 20 posts for bookmarks/dwell/first-hour replies; test 8-12 tweet videos with Premium; track impressions weekly for 2-4 week compounding.[1]
