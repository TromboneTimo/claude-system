# Brass instrument brand endorsement economics (Shires, Yamaha, Bach, Schagerl) + Japan website design rates 2026

## Key findings

**Brass instrument companies operate on a gear-and-exposure economy, NOT a cash-for-content economy.** Yamaha, S.E. Shires, Bach, and Schagerl offer endorsing artists substantial instrument discounts, custom shop access, website features, career advice, and global artist network exposure.
