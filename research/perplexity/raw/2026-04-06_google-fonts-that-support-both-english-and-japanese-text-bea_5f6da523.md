# Google Fonts that support both English and Japanese text beautifully. Best font pairings for bilingual English-Japanese websites 2025 2026. Premium editorial fonts with CJK support

## Key findings

**Noto Sans JP is the primary Google Font supporting both English (via Noto Sans) and Japanese (Hiragana, Katakana, Kanji) for bilingual websites, but it can appear generic for premium editorial or modern portfolio use due to its utilitarian design and large file size (9.2MB for Regular + Bold).** Other Google Fonts with Japanese/CJK support are limited; no 2025-2026 additions like Kedebideri, Stack Sans Text, Momo Trust Sans, Elms Sans, or Google Sans Flex explicitly mention CJK, focusing instead on Latin scripts.[1][2][9]
