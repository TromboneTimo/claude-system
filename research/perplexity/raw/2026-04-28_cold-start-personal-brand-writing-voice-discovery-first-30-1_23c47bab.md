# Cold-start personal brand writing voice discovery first 30-100 posts WRITE-PUBLISH-MEASURE-REFINE loop Justin Welsh Solopreneur OS Dickie Bush Ship 30 for 30

## Key findings

New personal-brand creators discover their **writing voice** through an iterative **WRITE-PUBLISH-MEASURE-REFINE** loop, starting from zero by producing 30-100 short-form posts (e.g., Atomic Essays or LinkedIn/Twitter threads) while analyzing engagement data to refine tone, structure, and topics.[1][2]

### Core Iterative Methodology (WRITE-PUBLISH-MEASURE-REFINE)
Creators like Justin Welsh emphasize a repeatable cycle for cold-start voice discovery, publicly documented in interviews and systems like his Solopreneur OS:
- **WRITE**: Generate ideas via ideation (e.g., personal stories, problems + solutions). Use simple structures: hook with pain/agitation, add intrigue via story, deliver solution, end with nugget + call-to-action. Tools like Hemingway App ensure grade-1 readability.[1][2]
- **PUBLISH**: Output daily/weekly short-form (e.g., 10 pieces from 1 long-form idea using hub-and-spoke model: 1 newsletter/podcast → 30 social posts).[1][2]
- **MEASURE**: Track what works (engagement, saves, comments) vs. doesn't; double down on compelling/polarizing formats.[2]
- **REFINE**: Cut failures, iterate on copywriting (e.g., read Ogilvy, Russell Brunson); practice communicating value until specific/viral balance emerges.[2][3]

This aligns with Dickie Bush's Ship 30 for 30 (30-day daily Atomic Essays for voice via rapid output), though specifics are absent here—creators report it forces iteration in first 30 posts.[Search results limited; general creator consensus from Welsh.]

### Posts Before Voice Stabilizes
30-100 posts. Welsh iterated early via daily LinkedIn/Twitter output, refining after testing formats/books; voice "stabilizes" via consistent high-output (e.g., 10 pieces/2 hours).[1][2] Bush's program targets 30 days; others like Sahil Bloom (Curiosity Chronicle origins), Pat Walls (Starter Story), Tim Denning (Medium journey), and Nicolas Cole (Ghostwriting Academy) document similar: voice emerges ~50-100 via volume, per public shares [no direct excerpts in results; inferred from creator patterns].[2]

### Signals of Found Voice
- **Engagement**: Likes/comments spike on polarizing/specific topics (e.g., solopreneur "enemies").[3]
- **Save rate**: High rereads on short nuggets.[2]
- **DM quality**: Inquiries shift to revenue-aligned (e.g., products/services).[3]
