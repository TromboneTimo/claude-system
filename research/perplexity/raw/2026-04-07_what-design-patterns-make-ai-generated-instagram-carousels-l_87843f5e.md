# What design patterns make AI-generated Instagram carousels look authentic vs obviously AI-made in 2025-2026? What are the telltale signs of AI-generated carousels that turn off aud

## Key findings

**AI-generated Instagram carousels in 2025-2026 appear authentic when using seamless panoramic layouts, bold high-contrast typography on the first slide, consistent brand templates, and hybrid photo-illustration styles that mimic hand-crafted imperfections like subtle overlaps or organic textures.** In contrast, obviously AI-made ones feature inconsistent aspect ratios, fuzzy images, generic layouts, and uniform perfection that lacks swipe-guiding flow, leading to poor algorithm performance and user skips.[1][2][3]
